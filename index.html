About SIBC Law College

Shri Indrapal Baburav Chaughule Law College (SIBC Law College) is the first law college in the rural area of Bhiwandi, started by Sai Seva Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, Bhiwandi, with the aim of bringing quality legal education to local students. The trust, established in 1991-92, has been working for social development through education. This college helps rural students gain legal knowledge without needing to travel far and focuses on both academic learning and moral values. Under the guidance of Principal Dr. Gayatri S. Patil, the college conducts various activities like moot courts, debates, and seminars to prepare students for successful careers in law and to become responsible citizens.

Programs Offered

1. Bachelor of Laws (LL.B.) - 3 Years

This program, also known as Legum Baccalaureus or LLB, is a three-year Bachelor of Laws degree. It is offered to aspirants who possess a graduate degree and is regulated and closely supervised by the Bar Council of India (BCI).

Eligibility:

  • Bachelor's Degree in any discipline from a recognized university with a minimum of 45% marks (40% for SC/ST candidates).
  • Successful completion of the Common Entrance Test (CET) conducted by the competent authority.

2. Bachelor of Arts & Bachelor of Laws (B.A. LL.B.) - 5 Years Integrated

This integrated program is a five-year course for candidates eligible to pursue after completing Class 12. It is an amalgamation of Humanities and Law streams, allowing candidates to study subjects such as History, Sociology, Administrative Law, Criminology, Family Law, and so on.

Eligibility:

  • 10+2 (or equivalent) examination with a minimum of 45% marks (40% for SC/ST candidates).
  • Successful completion of the Common Entrance Test (CET) conducted by the competent authority.

Courses & Syllabus

Bachelor of Laws (LL.B.) - 3 Years

This three-year program for graduates is designed to provide a comprehensive legal education. The curriculum is approved by the Bar Council of India and affiliated with the University of Mumbai.

Semester I

  • Labour Laws
  • Contract – I
  • Torts & Consumer Protection Law
  • Legal Language, Legal Writing & General English
  • Practical Training – I

Semester II

  • Law of Crimes
  • Constitutional Law
  • Family Law – I
  • Environmental Law
  • Practical Training/Drafting, Pleading & Conveyancing – I (DPC – I)

Semester III

  • Administrative Law
  • Family Law – II
  • Transfer of Property Act & Easement Act
  • Company Law
  • Practical Training – II

Semester IV

  • Jurisprudence (Legal Theory)
  • Contract – II
  • Constitutional Law - II
  • Elective A (choose one from Criminology, Taxation, Bankruptcy Laws)
  • Drafting, Pleading & Conveyancing – II (DPC – II)

Semester V

  • Bhartiya Nagarik Suraksha Sanhita (BNSS)
  • Civil Procedure Code (CPC) & Limitation Act
  • Labour Law - II
  • Public International Law & Human Rights
  • Practical Training – III

Semester VI

  • Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR) System
  • Bhartiya Sakshya Adhiniyam (BSA)
  • Elective B (choose two from a list of specialized topics)
  • Drafting, Pleading & Conveyancing – III (DPC – III)

Admission Process

  1. Application Form: Submission of the online/offline application form within the stipulated deadline.
  2. Common Entrance Test (CET): Appearing for the state-level CET for law admissions.
  3. Merit List: Preparation of a merit list based on CET scores and academic qualifications.
  4. Counseling & Document Verification: Shortlisted candidates will be called for counseling and verification of original documents.
  5. Admission Confirmation: Payment of fees and confirmation of admission.
